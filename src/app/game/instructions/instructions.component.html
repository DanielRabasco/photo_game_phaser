Different images will appear moving from the left side to the right side of the screen

 

Your mission is to type the first letter of each image before the image reach the end of the screen

 

The faster you type the letter the more points you get

 

Each wrong letter means less points, if you fail 4 times, you will get 0 points for this image

 

The more time you play the faster the images will move
